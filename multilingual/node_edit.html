When you cease to make a contribution you begin to die. I am only one, but still I am one. I cannot do everything, but still I can do something; and because I cannot do everything, I will not refuse to do something I can do. Eliminate something superfluous from your life. Break a habit. Do something that makes you feel insecure. Carry out an action with complete attention and intensity as if it were your last One machine can do the work of fifty ordinary men. No machine can do the work of one extraordinary man. A man may fall many times but he will not be a failure until he says someone pushed him. Do not play for safety; it is the most dangerous thing in the world.

Failure should be our teacher, not our undertaker. Failure is delay, not defeat. It is a temporary detour, not a dead end. Failure is something we can avoid only by saying nothing, doing nothing, and being nothing. One machine can do the work of fifty ordinary men. No machine can do the work of one extraordinary man. Until you try, you do not know what you cannot do. When you cease to make a contribution you begin to die. We can try to avoid making choices by doing nothing, but even that is a decision.

Eliminate something superfluous from your life. Break a habit. Do something that makes you feel insecure. Carry out an action with complete attention and intensity as if it were your last Nothing comes from doing nothing. Instead of seeing the rug being pulled from under us, we can learn to dance on a shifting carpet. Stop freewheeling, get into gear, and do something with your life. There are so many avenues to explore, so why not explore them? Never be afraid to step into the unknown, into the new. Do it fearlessly, always expecting the very best as you do so. People are always blaming their circumstances for what they are. I do not believe in circumstances. The people who get on in this world are the people who get up and look for the circumstances they want, and, if they cannot find them, make them.
